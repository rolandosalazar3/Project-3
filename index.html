Top 10 Colleges

In this study we are looking at the top ranked U.S. colleges and looking at the different factors that affect every different student recruit.

This information was found using data about Top Colleges from two different Kaggle sources that provided a CSV files. A Flask API was created from the combined data and is utilized on this website. Leaflet and Charts.js were used to plot the data images seen on this website.
